List of jobs needed:
- UI/UX Designer
As a UI/UX Designer, your role is to create engaging and intuitive user interfaces that enhance the overall user experience of websites, web applications, or mobile apps. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, including developers and stakeholders, to understand user needs, define design requirements, and translate them into visually appealing and functional designs.
- Front-End Developer
As a Front-End Developer, your primary responsibility is to build and implement visually appealing and interactive user interfaces for websites and web applications. You will work closely with UI/UX designers and back-end developers to bring designs to life using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
